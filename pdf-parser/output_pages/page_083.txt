Dystonic Reaction • Condition causing involuntary muscle movements or spasms typically of the face, neck and upper extremities. • Typically an adverse reaction to drugs such as Haloperidol (may occur with administration). • When recognized, administer Diphenhydramine 1 mg/kg up to 50 mg IM/IV. S.A.F.E.R. • Stabilize the situation by containing and lowering the stimuli. • Assess and acknowledge the crisis. • Facilitate the identification and activation of resources (chaplain, family, friends or police). • Encourage patient to use resources and take actions in his/her best interest. • Recovery or referral – leave patient in care of responsible person or professional, or transport to appropriate facility.
