Patients with automatic implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (AICD) will need external defibrillation if the AICD is ineffective. D. If defibrillation is needed on a patient with permanent implanted pacemaker, the defibrillator paddles or self-adhesive electrodes should be placed at least one inch from the pulse generator of the pacemaker, Initial attempt at pediatric defibrillation shall be at 2 J/kg. If unsuccessful, defibrillation should be attempted at 4 J/kg. Repeated defibrillations should be at > 4 J/kg to 10 J/kg until conversion occurs.
